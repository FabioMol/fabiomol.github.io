About

Plant invasions, islands, and humans.

Since my undergrads, I attempted to link plant ecology and biogeography with anthropogenic factors. This interest culminated in investigating the distribution of humans themselves during my PhD. In parallel, I kept working on plants, focusing on non-native species. Currently, my research areas are biological invasions and human geography.
